Frequently Asked Questions

What is your minimum order quantity?

For standard stock trays, our minimum order quantity is 1 pallet (approx. 5,000 units). For custom designs, the minimum is 50,000 units.

Do you offer free samples?

Yes, we offer complimentary sample kits of our standard products so you can test the quality and fit before placing a bulk order.

How long does custom packaging take?

Custom packaging usually takes 4-6 weeks from initial design approval to delivery, depending on the complexity of the mold.
